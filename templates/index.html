What makes it different

Built on verified records, not estimates dressed as facts.

The data integrity is the product. Three principles run through every screen.

1

Directly sourced

Certified TCAD appraisal rolls and Travis County Tax Office billing — loaded straight from official government exports. No third-party aggregators sitting between you and the record.

2

Confidence on every figure

Each number carries a visible label — Verified, Preliminary, Partial, Estimated, or Not Available. We never blend a guess with a recorded value, and missing data is shown as missing.

3

Traceable & dated

Every value points back to its source and as-of date. Estimates are visually distinct — dashed, muted, prefixed with ~ — so a projection is never mistaken for a recorded fact.

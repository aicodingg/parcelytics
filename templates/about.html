What Parcelytics Covers
■
Property Tax Intelligence

Full certified appraisal history for every Travis County parcel — market value, assessed value, taxable value, land and improvement breakdown, exemptions, and ownership. Data sourced directly from TCAD certified appraisal rolls, 2021–2026.

■
Tax Rate History

35 years of entity-level tax rates (1990–2025) across all Travis County taxing jurisdictions — Travis County, Austin ISD, City of Austin, municipal utility districts, and more. Track how each entity's rate has changed over time.

■
Investor Insight Reports

Automated insight reports for every parcel: year-over-year value growth, assessment ratio trends, homestead cap risk, large value jump detection, and 5-year tax projections. Built for acquisition underwriting, not casual browsing.

■
County Market Snapshot

County-wide preliminary market intelligence updated with each TCAD appraisal cycle. Median value changes by property type, county aggregate trends, and sector-specific breakdowns — Residential, Multi-Family, Commercial, Land, Agricultural.

■
Submarket Benchmarking

Compare any parcel against county medians and percentiles for its property type, use code, and neighborhood. Filter by sector, use code, year, and geography to understand where a parcel sits in the market.

■
2026 Preliminary Values

First-look at 2026 TCAD preliminary appraisal values for every Travis County parcel — clearly labeled as preliminary, subject to change at certification (July 25, 2026). Get ahead of the next appraisal cycle before values are locked.

Data Integrity Standard

Every figure on Parcelytics carries a confidence label — Verified Preliminary Estimated or Not Available. We never blend a guess with a real number. When data is missing, we say so.
